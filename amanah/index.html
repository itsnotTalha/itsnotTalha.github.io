About Amanah

Amanah is a secure traceability solution leveraging Blockchain and IoT technologies to combat counterfeit medicines in the pharmaceutical supply chain. Our platform provides end-to-end visibility, from manufacturing facilities to patient delivery, ensuring authenticity, compliance, and trust at every step.

By combining immutable distributed ledger technology with real-time environmental monitoring, Amanah creates an unprecedented level of transparency that protects public health, supports regulatory oversight, and strengthens Bangladesh's position in the global pharmaceutical market.

Technology Architecture

Four layers ensuring trust and efficiency

Blockchain Layer

Immutable record of all transactions and product movements, creating an unalterable audit trail that ensures data integrity and builds trust across the supply chain.

IoT Layer

Environmental sensor integration monitoring temperature, humidity, and location data throughout transit, ensuring pharmaceutical products maintain required storage conditions.

Smart Contract Layer

Automated compliance validation and execution of predefined business rules, streamlining operations while ensuring regulatory requirements are consistently met.

User Interface Layer

Accessible dashboard and mobile applications providing intuitive access to supply chain data, verification tools, and analytics for all stakeholders.
